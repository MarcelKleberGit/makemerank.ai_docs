## Overview

From the gaps in your analysis, MakeMeRank generates **content suggestions** – LinkedIn/B2B posts and long blog articles – tailored to your brand, topic, and the gaps found.

The texts are formulated in **your style**, not as generic AI output. MakeMeRank uses your **personas** and **content sources** for this.

For each suggestion, a **Perplexity Research Call** is run based on your **Primary Category** and **Brand Claim**, so current trends and developments are incorporated into the content.

## Why Content Generation Matters

Content created with your analysis gaps serves three goals:

1. **Citeable snippets** – Core statements are formulated so they work as standalone quotes or abstracts. When AI models surface your content, they can extract clear, quotable snippets instead of vague or fragmented text.
2. **Higher authority** – Content that addresses your actual gaps and uses E-E-A-T signals strengthens how AI perceives and cites you as a source.
3. **Better discoverability** – By targeting the intents and topics where you currently underperform, you increase the chance that AI models will find and recommend you in relevant queries.

## Context Weighting

You can fine-tune which context influences the suggestions. Move the point in the grid to weight **LinkedIn**, **Report**, **Content Sources**, or **New Topics** higher. The optional field below lets you add extra topic ideas or inspiration sources.

## Formats

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **LinkedIn / B2B Post** | Short, concise posts for social media – core statements formulated as standalone quotes |
| **Long Blog Article** | In-depth articles with structure – abstracts suitable for AI search snippets |

## Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add persona">
    Create a persona with description or sample text. Choose context tags for tone, role, and topic (e.g. Tech, Founder, Listicle). Each piece of content requires a persona.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use content sources">
    Connect LinkedIn or upload posts/articles. If you include them, they help produce thematically similar content, avoid duplicates, and supplement your story.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fine-tune context (optional)">
    Use the **Context weighting** grid to control which context the suggestions should weight higher: LinkedIn, Report, Content Sources, or New Topics. You can also add additional inspiration in the text field below.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate topic suggestions">
    Click **Generate topics**. MakeMeRank creates matching topics based on your analysis and context.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create post">
    Select a topic and click **Create post + image**. Optional: Enable Quality Pass, choose post image style.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit and use">
    Edit the draft as needed, copy the text, or save the image.
  </Step>
</Steps>
