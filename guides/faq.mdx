## General

### What is AI visibility and why does it matter?

AI visibility describes whether and how systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity mention, classify, and recommend you in their answers. As more search journeys move into AI interfaces, that visibility matters directly for demand and trust.

### What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It means optimizing your online presence specifically for AI-powered search and answer systems.

### Who is MakeMeRank for?

MakeMeRank is built for consultants, agencies, solo brands, experts, and companies that want to become more visible in AI answers.

## Usage

### How does the Quickscan work?

You enter a domain or a person with topic. MakeMeRank queries multiple AI models in parallel and returns a fast snapshot with model evidence, recognition, and early website or profile signals.

### How can I sign in?

You can currently sign in with **email/password**, **Google**, or **LinkedIn**.

### Which AI models are analyzed?

MakeMeRank currently uses **ChatGPT**, **Claude**, **Gemini**, and **Perplexity** across Quickscan and reporting flows.

### Is the Quickscan really free?

Yes. The Quickscan works without sign-up and without payment details. Anonymous usage is rate-limited.

## Pricing and subscription

### Can I cancel?

Yes. You can cancel self-serve subscriptions such as Pro or Agency at any time. See the [Terms of Use](https://makemerank.ai/terms) and [Withdrawal Policy](https://makemerank.ai/withdrawal) for details.

### What is Brand Insights?

Brand Insights compares the positioning your brand communicates with the perception AI search actually finds in quotes, model answers, and competitor signals. It helps you spot where your brand is already clearly associated with the right attributes and where competitors still have the stronger position.

## Content and style

### How does MakeMeRank learn my writing style?

Through **Personas** and **Content Sources**. Personas define tone, language, and perspective. Content Sources provide real context from existing posts, articles, files, or LinkedIn content.

### Are generated texts really written in my style?

Yes, if you use a persona. A persona is required for personalized content generation. Additional Content Sources improve quality even further, especially when they are maintained for the selected persona.

### Can I generate content without a report?

Partly, yes. If enough of your own context already exists, for example through LinkedIn or several stored pieces of content, you can generate topic ideas without active report context.
