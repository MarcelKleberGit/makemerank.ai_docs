## General

### What is AI visibility and why does it matter?

AI visibility describes whether and how AI models like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity mention and recommend your business in their answers. As more potential customers use AI assistants instead of Google, your AI visibility directly impacts inquiries and deals.

### What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the optimization of your online presence specifically for AI-powered search systems. While classic SEO targets Google rankings, GEO ensures ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other answer engines recognize you as an expert and recommend you.

### Who is MakeMeRank built for?

MakeMeRank is built for consultants, small businesses, agencies, and solo brands – anyone who wants to become visible in AI answers without needing a large marketing team or agency budget.

## Usage

### How does the MakeMeRank Quickscan work?

Enter your name or domain. MakeMeRank queries several AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity) with standardized questions and delivers four scores in under 2 minutes: Recognition, Visibility, Positioning, and Consistency.

### Which AI models are analyzed?

MakeMeRank analyzes your visibility in ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Gemini (Google), and Perplexity (Sonar). This gives you a complete picture of your presence across all relevant answer engines.

### Is the Quickscan really free?

Yes. The Quickscan requires no sign-up and no payment method. There is a rate limit per IP for anonymous use.

## Content & Style

### How does MakeMeRank learn my writing style?

Via **Personas** – you describe your style or add sample texts. Via **Content sources** – connect LinkedIn or upload posts/articles. MakeMeRank analyzes tone, sentence structure, and typical phrasing.

### Are the generated texts in my style?

Yes, when you use a persona. Personas are required for content generation – they combine your writing style with background/context (e.g. via tags like Tech, Founder, Listicle). Content sources (LinkedIn, uploads) provide additional context.
