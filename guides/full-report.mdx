## What the Full Report gives you

The Full Report is the real diagnosis behind the Quickscan. It shows not only **whether** you are visible, but **why** you are visible or invisible.

You get:

- a **score overview** with overall score, sub-scores, confidence, and spread
- three **analysis strands**: technical, visibility, and recognition
- **prioritized recommendations** with expected impact and effort
- a direct path into **content generation** based on your results

## Which questions the report answers

| Question | What the report shows |
|----------|------------------------|
| **Can AI understand my website or profile?** | The technical audit and clarity signals show how readable your signals are. |
| **Am I recommended without my brand name?** | The visibility analysis tests neutral queries without explicit brand seeding. |
| **How stable is model recognition of me?** | The recognition analysis shows recognition, confidence, and risk. |
| **What should I do next?** | The actions section prioritizes concrete next steps by impact and effort. |

## Domains and persons behave differently

| Scan type | What changes |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Domain** | Includes a technical audit with crawl, structure, and GEO signals |
| **Person** | No website audit, but a stronger focus on recognition and correct classification |

<Tip>
For person scans, always enter **name + topic**, for example `Jane Doe, B2B SEO`. Otherwise the visibility evaluation becomes less precise.
</Tip>

## How the Full Report works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the dashboard">
    You need a MakeMeRank account to run a Full Report.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start a report">
    Start a new report in the dashboard for a domain or person. You can optionally define a search focus. For person scans, LinkedIn can improve the context.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow the live progress">
    During the run, MakeMeRank shows progress across five steps: context, visibility, recognition, scoring, and recommendations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the results">
    Open the score overview, the three analysis strands, and the prioritized actions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Act immediately">
    From the report, you can move straight into content generation and create matching topic suggestions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
