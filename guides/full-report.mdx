## What is the Analysis?

The Analysis (Full Report) goes beyond the Quickscan. You get:

- a **cross-model analysis** – where each AI model mentions or overlooks you;
- a **technical audit** – llms.txt, robots.txt, Clarity score, structured data;
- **action recommendations** – prioritized measures with concrete impact potential;
- **content suggestions** – matching the identified gaps, in your style.

## Four Questions That Clarify Your AI Visibility

| Question | Answer in Report |
|----------|------------------|
| **Are my foundations in place?** | Technical audit + brand search: Is your website readable for AI? Do models find you in brand searches? |
| **Am I recommended without my brand name?** | Visibility without brand name: Are you mentioned in general queries too? |
| **What are my next steps?** | Prioritized measures with expected impact – derived directly from the analysis. |
| **What content do I need?** | Publish-ready drafts in your writing style – derived from your analysis gaps. |

## Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or log in to account">
    You need a MakeMeRank account for the analysis.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start analysis">
    Start an analysis via the dashboard. Optional: Connect LinkedIn or specify search focus for more precise results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Retrieve report">
    An analysis can take up to 90 seconds. Then open the report with scores, technical audit, visibility, and recommendations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Implement actions">
    Use the prioritized action list. Each recommendation is E-E-A-T-assigned (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) and traceable to findings from the analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout icon="circle-info" color="#EA580C">
The analysis considers your **content sources** and **personas** when you have LinkedIn connected or sample texts on file. This produces suggestions that fit you.
</Callout>
