## GEO Overview

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** means optimizing your presence for AI-powered search and answer systems – not just for classic search engines like Google.

More and more users ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, or Google AI instead of googling directly. When these models do **not** mention you, you lose potential customers – without noticing.

## GEO vs. SEO

| | SEO | GEO |
|---|-----|-----|
| **Goal** | Google rankings | Recommendations in AI answers |
| **Metrics** | Clicks, rankings, impressions | Visibility in model answers, citations |
| **Tools** | Search Console, Analytics | Prompt-based tests, brand monitoring |

GEO complements SEO – it’s not a replacement, but an additional dimension.

## AEO vs. GEO

- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):** Structure content so answer engines can extract it well (Q&A, lists, tables).
- **GEO:** Trust and visibility – that systems **choose** and **cite** your source.

Together they form a complete AI search strategy.

## Why GEO Matters

- **Zero-click problem:** AI often delivers complete answers without clicking your page – classic analytics barely show that.
- **Recommendation power:** Whoever appears as an expert in ChatGPT or Perplexity gains trust.
- **Early start:** Not everyone optimizes for AI yet – those who start early can secure advantages.
