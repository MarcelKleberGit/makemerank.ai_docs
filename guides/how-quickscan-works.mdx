## Overview

The Quickscan is the fast first layer before the Full Report. It does not build a full root-cause tree. Instead, it gives you a compact snapshot of how AI models currently recognize you and whether the most important basics are already in place.

## Technical flow

1. You enter a **domain** or a **person with topic**.
2. MakeMeRank launches multiple **parallel model queries**.
3. For **domains**, lightweight website signals are added.
4. The responses are condensed into a snapshot.
5. You see model evidence, recognition, and depending on the entity type either website basics or profile clarity.

## Which models are queried

The Quickscan queries four AI systems in parallel:

- **ChatGPT**
- **Claude**
- **Gemini**
- **Perplexity**

The goal is not only to see whether you are mentioned, but also how clearly the models can classify you.

## Which web signals are included for domains

For domain scans, MakeMeRank enriches the model responses with a lightweight technical check. This includes, among other things:

- `title`, `meta description`, and `H1`
- `Open Graph` data
- `canonical URL`
- `llms.txt`
- `Schema.org` / structured data
- `robots.txt` with AI bot access signals
- language, word count, HTTPS, and response time
- `meta robots`

These checks are intentionally lighter than in the Full Report. They are meant to show whether the most important foundations exist, not to explain every technical issue on your site.

## What happens for person scans

For person scans, there is no website audit. Instead, the Quickscan focuses on how consistently the models recognize you and whether they can classify your topic correctly.

<Tip>
For person scans, always enter `name + topic`. That is the only way MakeMeRank can distinguish between a simple name mention and an actual expert classification.
</Tip>

## How to read the result

The Quickscan mainly answers three questions:

- **Are you recognized by multiple models?**
- **Are the key website signals present?**
- **Is there already concrete evidence or are there obvious early gaps?**

What the Quickscan intentionally does **not** provide:

- a full root-cause analysis
- prioritized actions
- unseeded visibility without your brand name
- detailed content recommendations

That is exactly what the [Full Report](/guides/full-report) is for.
