## Overview

The **Quickscan** is fast and free. It measures your AI visibility with four scores in about 15 seconds. For domains, a light web probe is included; for persons, only the AI models are queried.

## Process

1. You enter a **domain** (e.g. `makemerank.ai`) or a **person** (name + topic).
2. MakeMeRank starts **parallel** queries:
   - **4 AI models** are queried with a standardized visibility prompt
   - **For domains:** A **web probe** is additionally performed (llms.txt, robots.txt, meta tags, Schema, etc.)
3. After at most **15 seconds** (or as soon as all models have responded), scores are aggregated.
4. You receive four scores: **Recognition**, **Visibility**, **Positioning**, **Consistency** – plus an **Overall score** (0–100).

## Web Signals (domains only)

For domain scans, a light **web probe** is also performed:

- **Homepage fetch** – Title, meta description, H1, Open Graph, canonical
- **llms.txt** – Present or not?
- **robots.txt** – Are AI bots (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended, PerplexityBot, anthropic-ai, cohere-ai) blocked or allowed?
- **Schema.org / JSON-LD** – Which types are present?
- **Word count**, **language** (lang attribute), **HTTPS**, **response time**

These signals feed into the **Clarity/Positioning score** (how well the website is readable for AI).

## Scoring

The four scores are queried per model and then **averaged** (successful models only):

- **Overall** ≈ average of Recognition, Presence, Clarity, and (100 − Risk)
- Models deliver structured JSON responses with `scores`, `reasoning`, and `known` (whether brand/person is recognized)
