## The Four Scores at a Glance

MakeMeRank delivers four core metrics. Together they form the **Overall Score** (0–100).

## Recognition

**Question:** Does AI find you right away? Do models mention you when explicitly searching for your name or brand?

- **High:** You are mentioned in relevant context – with correct assignment to topic and role.
- **Low:** Models barely know you or wrongly associate you with topics or people.

<Callout icon="lightbulb" color="#EA580C">
A high recognition score is the foundation. If models don't even mention you in brand searches, the biggest gap is here.
</Callout>

## Visibility (Presence)

**Question:** Does AI recommend you further? Do models mention you even for general queries without your name being mentioned?

- **High:** You appear in recommendation lists, expert queries, and thematic answers.
- **Low:** You are only found when directly searching for you.

This score is often the hardest to improve, but most important for lead generation.

## Positioning (Clarity)

**Question:** Does AI understand what you offer? How clear and structured is your content for AI models to read?

- **High:** Your website is technically and content-wise easy to capture – structured data, clear answer blocks, llms.txt, etc.
- **Low:** AI overlooks core content, structure is unclear, or key info is hidden (tabs, accordions).

## Consistency (Risk)

**Question:** Do AI facts about you agree? Are the statements consistent across different models?

- **High:** Your expertise, offerings, and positioning are described consistently.
- **Low:** Different or contradictory representations – e.g. various industries or roles.

<Callout icon="triangle-exclamation" color="#F7931A">
Contradictory info can confuse users. A Full Report helps you identify and fix such discrepancies.
</Callout>

## Scale 0–100

- **0–40:** Strong need for action – most models barely know or recommend you.
- **41–70:** Medium visibility – there are strengths, but also clear gaps.
- **71–100:** Good to very good AI visibility – you are perceived as a relevant source.

## Next Steps

- **Overall high, individual scores low?** Focus on the weakest areas.
- **Technical gaps?** The Full Report includes a technical audit with `llms.txt`, `robots.txt`, structure checks, and more detail.
- **Content ideas?** In the Pro plan MakeMeRank generates suggestions for LinkedIn and blog in your style.
