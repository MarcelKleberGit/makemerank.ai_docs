## Why Connect LinkedIn?

The LinkedIn integration helps MakeMeRank deliver **better analyses and content**:

- Existing posts are used as context
- Writing style and tone are captured
- More precise content suggestions – less generic

## Setting Up the Connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open profile">
    Go to **Profile** → **Content Sources** in the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Connect with LinkedIn">
    Follow the button to the OAuth flow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize on LinkedIn">
    LinkedIn asks for permissions. MakeMeRank only **reads** content – no posts can be written or published through the integration.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Load posts">
    After connecting you can **Load posts** and add them as content sources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Content Sources: Options

Content sources – from LinkedIn, uploads, crawls, or free text – are **automatically summarized and prepared** so they can be used ideally as context for analyses and content generation. They are incorporated into generation **at your request**, for example to produce thematically similar articles, avoid duplicate suggestions, or supplement and extend your existing story. No other tool provides this much context in one place – which saves you many work steps.

Besides LinkedIn you can add content in other ways:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Free text** | Paste post text or article excerpt |
| **Crawl blog** | Enter URL – LinkedIn articles and blog URLs are read in |
| **Upload file** | TXT, Word, PDF – content is automatically summarized |

## Privacy

- **Read-only** – No posts can be written or published through the integration; only reading for context. A quality-of-life feature that keeps you in control.
- We only store the access token – your data stays with you
- All retrieved information is publicly accessible anyway
- You can disconnect at any time
