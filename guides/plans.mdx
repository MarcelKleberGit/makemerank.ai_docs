## Plan overview

The pricing UI currently offers four options: **Free**, **Pro**, **Agency**, and **Consulting**.

| Feature | Free | Pro | Agency |
|---------|------|-----|--------|
| **Price** | €0 | €29/month or €249/year | €200/month or €2000/year |
| Quickscan | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Full Reports | 1 once on signup | 4/month | 100/month |

| Full detail insights | – | ✓ | ✓ |

## Free

- **Unlimited quick scans** – Quickscan works without login
- **1 Full Report on signup** – One deeper analysis to get started
- **Top 3 action items** – First priorities without upgrading
- **Content ideas preview** – Early look at the content workflow

## Pro

`Pro` is available as:

- **€29/month**
- **€249/year**

Included in Pro:

- **4 Full Reports / month**
- **12 content suggestions / month**
- **12 article generations / month**
- **4 Brand Insights analyses / month**
- **All detailed insights + prioritized actions**
- **Writing styles and Content Sources**
- **LinkedIn import with real profile context**

## Agency

`Agency` is available as:

- **€200/month**
- **€2000/year**

Included in Agency:

- **100 Full Reports / month**
- **200 content suggestions / month**
- **200 article generations / month**
- **100 Brand Insights analyses / month**
- **The same core product features as Pro with much higher limits**
- **A better fit for client delivery, repeated audits, and agency new-business work**

## Consulting

The pricing section also includes a **Consulting** offer starting from **€149**.

Consulting includes:

- personal analysis of your AI visibility
- a 1:1 GEO strategy session
- a prioritized action plan with quick wins
- delivery by a real agency team

## Limits in detail

- **Quickscan** – Available for free without login
- **Free plan** – 1 Full Report on signup
- **Pro plan** – 4 Full Reports, 12 content suggestions, 12 article generations, and 4 Brand Insights analyses per month
- **Agency plan** – 100 Full Reports, 200 content suggestions, 200 article generations, and 100 Brand Insights analyses per month
- **Consulting** – Custom scope, not a fixed self-serve credit bundle

You can see your current usage after login in the subscription area of the app.
