## Plan overview

The pricing UI currently offers three options: **Free**, **Pro**, and **Consulting**.

## Self-serve plans

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| **Price** | €0 | €29/month or €249/year |
| Quickscan | ✓ | ✓ |
| Full Reports | 1 once on signup | 4/month |
| Top action items | ✓ | ✓ |
| Full detail insights | – | ✓ |
| Content ideas preview | ✓ | – |
| Content suggestions | – | 12/month |
| Article generations | – | 12/month |
| Personas / writing styles | – | ✓ |
| Content Sources | – | ✓ |
| LinkedIn import | – | ✓ |

## Free

- **Unlimited quick scans** – Quickscan works without login
- **1 Full Report on signup** – One deeper analysis to get started
- **Top 3 action items** – First priorities without upgrading
- **Content ideas preview** – Early look at the content workflow

## Pro

`Pro` is available as:

- **€29/month**
- **€249/year**

Included in Pro:

- **4 Full Reports / month**
- **12 content suggestions / month**
- **12 article generations / month**
- **All detailed insights + prioritized actions**
- **Writing styles and Content Sources**
- **LinkedIn import with real profile context**

## Consulting

The pricing section also includes a **Consulting** offer starting from **€149**.

Consulting includes:

- personal analysis of your AI visibility
- a 1:1 GEO strategy session
- a prioritized action plan with quick wins
- delivery by a real agency team

## Limits in detail

- **Quickscan** – Available for free without login
- **Free plan** – 1 Full Report on signup
- **Pro plan** – 4 Full Reports, 12 content suggestions, and 12 article generations per month
- **Consulting** – Custom scope, not a fixed self-serve credit bundle

You can see your current usage after login in the subscription area of the app.
