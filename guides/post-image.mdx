Post images are **automatically generated** using Nano Banano Pro, based on predefined settings. You can choose the image style when creating a post.

## Key concepts for content generation

- **Analysis** – Start an analysis first so content suggestions are tailored to your gaps
- **Persona** – Required. Every piece of content is based on a persona. A persona combines **writing style** and **background/context**, configured via tags (e.g. Tech, Founder, Listicle)
- **Content Sources** (optional) – Connect LinkedIn or upload posts/articles. They can be included in generation to produce thematically similar articles, avoid duplicate suggestions, or supplement your story
