## What you get from the Quickscan

The Quickscan is your fastest entry point into MakeMeRank. It is free, does not require sign-up, and gives you an initial evidence snapshot of your AI visibility.

<Info>
The Quickscan is intentionally a snapshot, not a final ranking. For root causes, priorities, and concrete actions, you need the Full Report.
</Info>

## What is checked

What you see depends on whether you scan a domain or a person:

| Area | Domain scan | Person scan |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **AI recognition** | Shows how many models recognize you | Shows how clearly models can classify you |
| **Website basics / profile clarity** | Highlights key technical and content signals from your website | Shows whether your profile is understood consistently across models |
| **Evidence from models** | Shows how individual models describe you or fail to find you | Shows how individual models classify you or overlook you |

For domain scans, lightweight website checks are also included, such as `title`, `meta description`, `H1`, `llms.txt`, `schema`, `canonical`, `robots.txt`, language, HTTPS, and response time.

## Which models are used

The Quickscan queries four AI systems in parallel:

- **ChatGPT**
- **Claude**
- **Gemini**
- **Perplexity**

## How the Quickscan works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter a domain or person">
    Enter a domain like `makemerank.ai` or, for person scans, always use **name + topic**, for example `Jane Doe, B2B SEO`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optionally add LinkedIn">
    For person scans, you can optionally connect LinkedIn to provide additional context. You can also start without it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the Quickscan">
    MakeMeRank runs the model queries in parallel and, for domains, adds lightweight website signals.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the snapshot">
    You immediately see which models find you, how clearly you are classified, and which early signals on your site are present or missing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to move straight to the Full Report

The Full Report is the right next step if you want to:

- understand **why** you are not being recommended
- get concrete **action recommendations**
- test your **visibility without your brand name**
- understand the **technical causes** behind weak signals
- create **content suggestions** directly from the findings

For the methodology behind the snapshot, see [How Quickscan Works](/guides/how-quickscan-works). If you want to understand the deep-dive analysis, see [Full Report](/guides/full-report) and [How Full Report Works](/guides/how-analysis-works).
