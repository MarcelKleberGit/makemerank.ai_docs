## Overview

The Quickscan gives you a first overview of your AI visibility in about 2 minutes. It's free and requires no sign-up.

## What is checked?

MakeMeRank queries various AI models with standardized questions about your domain or person and evaluates the answers against four criteria:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Recognition** | Does AI find you right away? |
| **Visibility** | Does AI recommend you further? |
| **Positioning** | Does AI understand what you offer? |
| **Consistency** | Do AI facts about you agree? |

## Supported AI Models

The Quickscan uses:

- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Google AI / Gemini**
- **Perplexity** (Sonar)

## Steps for Quickscan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose domain or person">
    Enter either your domain (e.g. `makemerank.ai`) or your name plus topic (e.g. "John Smith, B2B SEO").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional: Connect LinkedIn">
    With a connected LinkedIn profile we improve the analysis through more context. You can also start without LinkedIn.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start scan">
    Click **Scan**. The queries run in parallel and typically take 1–2 minutes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review results">
    You see the four scores plus a short summary. The Full Report shows details for each model.
  </Step>
</Steps>
