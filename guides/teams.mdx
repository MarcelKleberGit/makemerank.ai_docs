## What Teams does

The **Team** area in the dashboard lets the **account owner** invite other users by email. After someone joins, the whole group works in one shared workspace: the same scans, Full Reports, Brand Insights, Query Fan Out results, and content data. **Usage limits** (reports, Brand Insights runs, content credits, and so on) apply to that **shared account**, not per person.

Billing, subscription changes, and sending invitations stay with the **owner**. Members use the owner’s plan and quotas while they are on the team.

## Who can do what

| Role | Typical actions |
|------|-----------------|
| **Owner** | Invites members by email, sees pending **email invitations** (for people who do not have an account yet), can remove members |
| **Member** | Sees the same analyses and results as the owner; can **leave** the team; can remove **only themselves** from the team (not other members) |

Only the owner can invite new members.

## How invitations work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Owner enters an email">
    In **Dashboard → Team**, the owner adds an address and sends an invitation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Existing or new user">
    If the person already has a MakeMeRank account, they get a flow to join the team. If not, an **email invitation** is created; those links expire after **14 days**, and the owner can **revoke** a pending email invite from the Team page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept or decline">
    Open invitations appear under **Open invitations**. The invitee can **accept** or **decline**. After acceptance, they work in the owner’s account like other members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If an automatic invitation email cannot be sent, the invitation is still stored: the recipient can see it after sign-in under **Team**.

## One team per user

A user account can only be part of **one** team at a time (as owner or member). You cannot belong to two different shared accounts in parallel. Someone who already belongs to another team’s account cannot be invited until that situation is resolved.

## Where to find it

After login: **Dashboard → Team** (path in the app: `/dashboard/team`).

For limits that apply to the whole workspace (Full Reports, Brand Insights, and so on), see [Plans & Limits](/guides/plans).
