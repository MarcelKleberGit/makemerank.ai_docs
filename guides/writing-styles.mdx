## What are Personas?

Personas are **required** for content generation – every piece of content is based on a persona. A persona combines **writing style** and **background/context**, configured via tags (e.g. Tech, Founder, Listicle). This ensures suggestions sound like you and match your positioning.

## Creating a Persona

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Personas">
    In the dashboard under **Personas** or **Content** → Writing style configuration.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create new persona">
    Click **Create new persona**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name and description">
    Enter a name (e.g. "My LinkedIn style") and describe your style or add a sample text.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose context tags">
    Select tags that describe your persona: tone (personal, professional, humorous), role (expert, consultant, founder), topic (tech, marketing, career), etc.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional: AI analysis">
    Use **Create persona** to have your text summarized and optimized by AI. You can use the AI summary or the original text.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set as default">
    If you have multiple personas, mark one as default for content generation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Context Tags

The tags help the AI weight your persona better:

- **Tone:** Personal, Professional, Humorous, Inspiring, Factual
- **Role:** Expert, Thought Leader, Consultant, Founder
- **Topic:** Surveys, Career, Leadership, Tech, Marketing, Storytelling
- **Format:** Listicle, How-to, Opinion

## Tonality in Profile

In your **Profile** you can additionally set a **default tonality** (e.g. Professional, Casual, Witty, Direct) and a **tonality sample text**. These feed in as a basis for personal content.
