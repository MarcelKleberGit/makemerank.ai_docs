## First Steps

The Quickscan shows you in about 2 minutes whether AI models recommend you – free and without registration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the app">
    Open [makemerank.ai](https://makemerank.ai) in your browser.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter domain or person">
    Enter your domain (e.g. `makemerank.ai`) or your name plus topic (e.g. "John Smith, SEO consultant in Berlin"). For person scans, name + topic helps achieve more precise results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Quickscan">
    Click **Scan** and wait about 1–2 minutes. MakeMeRank uses ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI, and Perplexity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review results">
    You see four scores: **Recognition**, **Visibility**, **Positioning**, and **Consistency**. Details are in [Interpreting Scores](/guides/interpreting-scores).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next Steps

After the Quickscan you can:

- **Request an Analysis (Full Report)** – for deeper insights, action recommendations, and content suggestions (Pro or Single Report).
- **Create an account** – to save reports, use content sources, and generate premium content.
- **Compare plans** – [Plans & Limits](/guides/plans) overview.

<Callout icon="lightbulb" color="#EA580C">
The Quickscan is free and requires no sign-up. Use it repeatedly to track progress over time.
</Callout>
