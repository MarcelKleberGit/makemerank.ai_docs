## Your fast start

The Quickscan is free and works without sign-up. It gives you a snapshot of how well AI models currently recognize your domain or profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Open [makemerank.ai](https://makemerank.ai).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter a domain or person">
    Enter your domain such as `makemerank.ai` or, for person scans, always use **name + topic**, for example `Jane Doe, SEO consultant in Berlin`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the Quickscan">
    Start the scan. MakeMeRank queries ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity in parallel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the snapshot">
    You see which models find you, how clearly you are classified, and which early website or profile signals are visible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What usually comes next

- **Open the Full Report** for root causes, weighting, and prioritized actions
- **Create an account** to save reports and generate content. You can sign in with email, Google, or LinkedIn.
- **Use Brand Insights** if you want to compare your intended positioning with how AI search currently describes you and competitors
- **Try Query Fan Out** after a Full Report to see internal follow-up queries behind a user question ([guide](/guides/query-fan-out))
- **Invite your team** if several people should share one account and the same limits ([Teams](/guides/teams))
- **Set up Personas and Content Sources** for more personalized topic suggestions and drafts
- **Compare plans** in [Plans & Limits](/guides/plans)

<Info>
The Quickscan is an entry point, not a final diagnosis. For a stable, actionable view, move into the Full Report afterward.
</Info>
